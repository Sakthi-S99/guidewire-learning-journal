## Hi, I'm Sakthi 👋

I'm a Senior Software Engineer specializing in **Guidewire BillingCenter** — one of the most financially complex platforms in the insurance industry. I work at the intersection of insurance domain knowledge and technical implementation, helping carriers configure, extend, and integrate the Guidewire suite.

My day-to-day spans bug fixes and root cause analysis, configuration and Gosu development, production support, architecture design, and cross-suite integrations. This journal is where I document what I learn — so it's useful to me, and hopefully to others working in the same space.
