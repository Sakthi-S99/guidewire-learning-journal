## How to Add a New Certification

1. Get the badge image URL from the issuer (Credly, Badgr, etc.)
2. Copy the template block from comments above
3. Fill in: cert name, badge URL, issued date, credential ID, verify link
4. Place it under the right section
5. `git push` — it goes live automatically

!!! tip "Credly Badges"
    Most Guidewire and Red Hat certs issue badges via [Credly](https://credly.com).
    On Credly → open your badge → Share → copy the **Image URL** and **Public URL** for the template above.
