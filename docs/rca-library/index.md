# RCA Library

Root cause analyses from real production and UAT incidents.

---

## How to Use

1. Copy [template.md](template.md)
2. Rename: `YYYY-MM-DD-short-description.md`
3. Place in `rca-library/incidents/`
4. Add entry to the table below

---

## Incident Log

| Date | Component | Severity | Summary |
|---|---|---|---|
| _YYYY-MM-DD_ | _Module_ | _P1/P2/P3_ | _[Short description](incidents/filename.md)_ |

---

!!! tip "Tip"
    Writing RCAs within 24 hours of resolution gives the best recall. Even a 10-minute write-up is valuable.
